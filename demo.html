About us

Welcome to Cafe Affogato, where the artistry of coffee meets the elegance of indulgence. Nestled in the heart of the vibrant city, our journey began with a simple passion for crafting exceptional coffee experiences. Founded in 2010 by coffee aficionado Emma Bennett, Cafe Affogato has evolved into a haven for connoisseurs and casual coffee lovers alike. Our baristas, each with a minimum of five years' experience, meticulously select and roast the finest coffee beans from exotic locales, infusing a sense of adventure into every cup.


Beyond our meticulously crafted coffees, our menu boasts an array of sumptuous treats, from artisanal gelato to tantalizing pastries, all curated to complement the exquisite flavors of our brews. At Cafe Affogato, we aspire to elevate your coffee rendezvous into a sensory symphony, one cup at a time. Join us, and let's savor the blend of tradition and innovation, where every visit is a journey of taste and discovery.

Our Speciality

Indulge in our signature Affogato, a sublime fusion of rich espresso and creamy gelato, creating a symphony of flavors that's pure perfection in a glass.
